# Experiment: Sequence length ablation (10 vs 20 vs 50)

## Hypothesis
Longer history may improve AUC but can increase cost.

## Setup
- Synthetic quick experiment (local) to validate weekly experiment loop
- Same generator/model except seq_len
- Metrics: AUC, LogLoss (+ label-shuffle sanity AUC)

## Results
| seq_len | AUC | LogLoss | Label-shuffle AUC |
|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| 10 | 0.547411 | 0.692533 | 0.502227 |
| 20 | 0.551867 | 0.689321 | 0.504291 |
| 50 | 0.553399 | 0.688647 | 0.503928 |

## Key Insight
- As seq_len increases, model’s AUC slightly improves and LogLoss decreases —  
  indicating that more history improves predictive signal.
- Label-shuffle AUC ≈ 0.50 confirms the model isn’t learning random noise.

## Takeaways
- “seq_len이 늘수록 AUC↑, LogLoss↓ (개선폭은 작지만 일관됨)”
